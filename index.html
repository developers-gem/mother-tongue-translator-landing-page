Rationale For African Language Translation App

This is not only a tech idea but purpose-driven app to unlock cultural, social and economic potentials of this great continent.

1. Economic Opportunity

Africa’s digital economy is growing fast, but language limits adoption. Ability to communicate without language barriers will definitely promote inter-ethnic trade and commercial activities.

2. Digital Inclusion and Equity

A language translation app promotes sense of belonging and confidence in taking advantage of technology.

3. Social Inclusion

Ability to understand one another’s language promotes social interaction across various ethnic groups

4. International business opportunities

A multi-ethnic-based translation app promotes intra-Africa business opportunities.

5. Cultural Preservation and Language Survival

An African-Language translation app reduces the potential for language extinction due to lack of digital presence.

6. Education and Literacy

A translation app can support bilingual education, help students translate learning materials and assists teachers in multilingual classrooms.

7. frican Diaspora and Global Use

The African Diaspora community wants to teach their children native languages, to communicate with family back home, so this app is a cultural reconnection tool.
